Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Fanivo is built for football fans. We collect only what we need to run the app and never sell your data.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (if you sign up with email)
  • Display name and username
  • Profile photo (if you upload one)

Usage Data

  • Match predictions you submit
  • Teams and players you follow
  • Leagues you create or join
  • Watch parties you host or RSVP to

Device Information

  • Device type and iOS version (for crash reporting)
  • Approximate location (for watch party discovery — optional)
  • Push notification token (if you enable notifications)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Fanivo app
  • To run prediction leagues and leaderboards
  • To send match alerts and notifications (only if you opt in)
  • To display your profile in leagues you join
  • To process in-app purchases via Apple
  • To show relevant watch parties near you (location is never stored)

3. Third-Party Services

Fanivo uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, and storage. Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob — ads shown to free users only. Google Privacy Policy
  • API-Football — live match data. No personal data is shared.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers. We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within 30 days. Prediction history and league scores may be retained in anonymized form.

5. Advertising

Free users may see ads served by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalized based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized ads in your iOS Settings under Privacy → Tracking.

Premium subscribers (Tournament Pass or Annual) see no ads.

6. Children's Privacy

Fanivo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at iryamukurup@yahoo.fr

8. International Users

Fanivo is operated from Belgium. If you are located outside Belgium, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries where our service providers operate, including the United States. By using Fanivo you consent to this transfer.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or by email. Continued use of Fanivo after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
